The Basics to Gaming.

There are many people playing online games in this new era. So now let me teach you about the basics to gaming.

To start playing online games with your friends, you will need to install many different applications. So make sure your computer or device will have enough storage. Most games typically use up around 1gb-10gb (giga byte) or even up to 40gb.

The most popular gaming application that is used by many is called Steam. You can search up steam in google and install it. Inside steam there are hundreds or even thousands of different user created games for new players to explore and enjoy. But you have to remember that some of these games you have to pay an amount of money to be able to play.
